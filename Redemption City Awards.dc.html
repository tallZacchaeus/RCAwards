About the Awards

An evening built to honour the people who make a city extraordinary.

The Redemption City Awards exist to spotlight excellence in every form it takes — the entrepreneur who took the risk, the organization that raised the bar, the volunteer who quietly changed lives, and the creative who moved a room to its feet.

Our vision is simple: to build a living legacy of achievement that inspires the next generation to aim higher. Each honouree becomes part of a story the whole community shares.

"Excellence, when it is seen, multiplies."
